The International Society for Machine Learning in Economics (ISMLE) is pleased to announce its 2026 Annual Conference. We invite submissions of high-quality research papers at the intersection of machine learning, computational methods, and economic theory. The conference aims to foster dialogue between academic researchers, central bank economists, and industry data scientists to advance the frontier of economic modeling. 国际机器学习与经济学协会 (ISMLE) 很高兴地宣布其 2026 年度会议。我们诚邀处于机器学习、计算方法与经济学理论交叉领域的高质量研究论文投稿。本次会议旨在促进学术界研究员、央行经济学家与工业界数据科学家之间的对话，共同推进经济建模的前沿发展。

Research Tracks征稿主题方向

We welcome submissions in, but not limited to, the following areas:

我们欢迎（但不限于）以下领域的论文投稿：

  • Computational Macroeconomics: Deep learning applications for analyzing DSGE and heterogeneous agent models.
  • Machine Learning in Econometrics: High-dimensional inference, causal machine learning, and non-parametric estimation.
  • Market Design & AI: Algorithmic pricing, mechanism design, and automated market makers.
  • Reinforcement Learning in Economics: RL for policy design and agent-based modeling.
  • Natural Language Processing: LLMs for economic modeling, central bank communications and sentiment analysis.
